The Clone Wars

What is Clone Wars?

The Clone Wars is a very popular Star Wars series created by George Lucas and Dave Filoni. It had 133 episodes in 7 seasons and was aired for 12 years.Its great success was due to its unique take on the Star Wars franchise. The series takes place between "Attack of the Clones" and "Revenge of the Sith". When these movies came out, there was a large chunk of the timeline that was empty. The Clone Wars took advantage of this and filled the gap. The show introduced dozens of new characters and made the fictious galaxy more alive. It was incredebly violent for a kids show and gave fan favorites more screen time before they died in the prequel movies. It made clones relatable and made Order 66 a much more impactful moment. This sereis also gave rise to many others that wouldn't make sense without this show as context.

My Personal Thoughts

I have watched this show for many years and will probably rewatch it again in the future. It introduced some of my favorite characters in all of Star Wars. It also had some of the best action scenes I have ever seen in a Star Wars series. I loved the story telling style, as well as the sheer variety in storylines. It filled the hole between the second and third movies that would have hurt the franchise if it was left unfilled. Some episodes were on military campaigns and others on politics, preventing it from being stale. I love this show and will rewatch it for years to come.

Characters

Jedi
  • Anakin Skywalker: The Chosen One who was destined to bring balance to the force since the first movie. He and the other two jedi here were present in most of the episodes.
  • Obi-Wan Kenobi: Anakin's master and friend. His past is explained in more detail in this show. We meet his love and run into his rival.
  • Ahsoka Tano: Padawan of Anakin Skywalker. She debuts in this show and goes on to appear in other series, as well as receive her own show.
Clones
  • Captain Rex: Captain of the 501st. He served under Anakin and Ahsoka, quickly becoming a friend to both Jedi. He leaves the Republic before Order 66.
  • Commander Cody: This clone was depicted in the third movie and served alongside General Kenobi. He led the 212th Battalion. He is present during Order 66.
Villans
  • Count Dooku: The apprentice of Darth Sidious and leader of the Separtist Alliance. Dooku is the main antagonist to the Republic, since no one knew the identity of Darth Sidious.
  • General Grievous: A brave warrior brainwashed and augmented by the Sith, Grievous develops a strong hatred for Jedi and becomes incredebly skilled with a lightsaber. He commands the Separtist Droid Army.
  • Darth Maul: Originally believed to have died in the first movie, Maul comes back with a vengeance against Kenobi and tries to establish a criminal empire.

Top 3 Favorite Arcs

  • Umbara: This arc is heavily focused on the clones of the 501st Legion as they find themselves serving under Jedi Pong Krell on Umbara, a hostile planet where they are fighting the natives. Krell later betrays them.
  • Grievous' Lair: This arc follows Jedi Kit Fisto and his padawan as they find themselves in the lair of General Grievous. Only Fisto escapes the horrorific labrynth after Grievous kills his padawan and clone escort.
  • Savage Opress: Created by the Night Sisters, a coven of space witches, Savage is given the task to kill Dooku. After failing, he is sent to find Maul. When he does, they team up and begin their criminal empire. Savage is killed when the duo fight Sidious.
